Minecraft Calculator

Minecraft Calculator

Minecraft Construction Calculator

About a year ago, I came up with an idea to make a spreadsheet that calulates how many blocks you need to build a Minecraft Structure. I built my spreadsheet, but there is not much you can do with a spreadsheet. So I decided to take it up with C++. I never did accomplish that goal. I am now making an HTML5 program. I am having a lot of fun.

The first thing I am developing is the basic structure of the program, it is quite easy, I have done it in C++ and Python and even with Excel. Then I will go into more advanced things that will make it easier to use with more customization. Come on, it is easy to make a program that can calculate a cube, but something that could calulate many different aspects of a Minecraft structure is amazing. Well, I might be pushing it too much, but I am excited to make it. No doubt about that. I am just learning as I go with this one.

Browser Extension
APOD Extension

APOD Extension

Astronomy Picture of the Day Browser Extension

The astronomy picture of the day is an astronomy picture that is put out by NASA every day. These pictures (and somethimes videos) are facinating and I really enjoy browsing them. In the Chrome Webstore there are many different browser extensions for the astronomy picture of the day, but they are either for a new tab, or aren't pretty good, but they are olay. However, in AMO (Firefox Addons) there were none. Which really surprised me, so I decided to make my own.

The one that I made is a simple browser popup that can present the current Astronomy Picture of the Day and previous ones.

Get it at AMO. See it on GitHub.
