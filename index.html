Heb12

Heb12 is a project I set up in April 2018 to build Bible apps for all sorts of devices. Heb12 is short for Hebrews 4:12, which reads

For the word of God is living, and active, and sharper than any two-edged sword, and piercing even to the dividing of soul and spirit, of both joints and marrow, and quick to discern the thoughts and intents of the heart.

God's word is so powerful, and Heb12's goal is to bring the word of God to easily be read in all sorts of devices as a free (libre) and open source software.

There is currently only a desktop app for Heb12 that runs on Windows and GNU/Linux. You can get it at the GitHub releases page.

LibreCheck

LibreCheck is a easy and simple child check in program for places like churches and daycares. A lot of software is not only complex and hard to use, but are also prone to bugs.

It is in its early stages in development, but you can go to its GitHub page for more information. I might use LibreCheck to earn money for Heb12 by selling it to churches.

BibleCompare

This is a simple Bible chapter comparing website I made in a few minutes to figure out whether 2nd or 3rd John was longer. It compares characters and words.
